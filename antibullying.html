The 2013/2014 NG Anti-bullying Campaign: Silence Is Consent.

Why an anti-bullying lesson again this year? Bullying continues to be a life-altering issue for teens across the country, often with long-term consequences. Some teens will feel compelled to take drastic measures, such as carrying weapons for protection or seeking violent revenge. Others, in desperation, even consider suicide. Researchers have found that years later, long after the bullying has stopped, adults still experience the effects of bullying. We all have power to influence aspects of our school culture relating to bias, hate, and bullying. Hopefully this campaign will start some critical conversations for our school to act on! Our focus this year is the bystander and the power they have in these situations.
